India continues to be an agrarian society , notwithstanding recent developments in engineering and technology computer science and space science . The Faculty of Agriculture , Usha Martin University has been established to play a flagship role in turning rural Jharkhand and people to first rate agricultural entrepreneurs and agri - business managers . The impact of its agriculture programmes will help regenerate agricultural fervor in years to come , by equipping graduates with skills for higher per acre yields / earnings . This should also help alleviate poverty and ensure livelihood security in a new eco - system . BSc . ( Hons . ) in Agriculture is a technical degree program where agriculture sciences , basic sciences , interdisciplinary and allied sciences are integrated to develop knowledge of scientific and technical aspects of agriculture . Education and experiential learning enable learners to acquire scientific knowledge and skills to contribute for development of tools / technologies or processes to increase both production and productivity of agriculture . UMU proposes to impart through its Faculty of Agriculture , technology supported education for futuristic agri - practices through customized curriculum .
